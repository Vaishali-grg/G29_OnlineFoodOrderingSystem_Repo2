Frequently Asked Questions

All your meal-time queries answered!

What are the canteen operating hours? ▲
The campus canteen is open from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekdays and 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekends.
How do I place an order? ▼
Just log in to the CampusBites app, choose your favorite dishes, and place an order. Pickup or delivery — your choice!
What payment options are available? ▼
We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and your student wallet linked to your college ID.
How long does food preparation take? ▼
Food is usually delivered within 15–25 minutes, depending on kitchen load and your location on campus.
Can I cancel or change my order? ▼
You can cancel or modify your order within 2 minutes of placing it. After that, it might already be cooking!
Who do I contact for help? ▼
Use the "Support" section in the app to chat with our team, or visit the help desk at the canteen entrance.
